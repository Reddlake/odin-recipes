Powdered Sugar Donuts

Soft and Delicious Donuts

These powdered sugar donuts will absolutely blow your mind right out of the oven or even a few days later!

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  2. 1 cup white sugar
  3. 1 cup butter
  4. 1 cup vegetable oil
  5. 1 pinch salt
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  8. 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  9. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  10. 4 cups all-purpose flour
